About Our Chefs

Gustave Berneir

Executive Chef
Cooking does not tolerate distraction. If you enter this arena without due respect.

If given to you, then you will not be forever a chef in supermarket cooking, but you will become a pro with a name, perhaps even a chef with a capital letter. Be a real pro, and they will know about you. By the way, this is not the main thing.

Riccardo Rinaldi

Executive Chef
High culinary art is the only human ability about which nothing bad can be said.

With food, everything is simple: we are all doomed to eat three times a day for the rest of our lives. So let's eat what is nutritious, tasty, and enjoyable. In my opinion, a very uncomplicated philosophy. Everyone can cook with enthusiasm.

Alessio Costa

Executive Chef
Cooking is like love: you either need to plunge into it with your head, or not to plunge at all.

Everyone can cook with enthusiasm. You just need to be a person inspired and passionate, try to enjoy the cooking process. And when you have a good soulful dish and all your efforts are appreciated, that will be enough for you to want to cook more.
